Research

My research focuses on understanding the early Universe and the physics encoded in the Cosmic Microwave Background (CMB).

Current Interests

  • CMB polarization and foreground separation
  • Kinematic Sunyaev–Zel'dovich effect
  • Axions and beyond-ΛCDM models

Ongoing Projects

I am currently working on analyzing high-resolution CMB data to constrain new physics using power spectrum and cross-correlation techniques.
