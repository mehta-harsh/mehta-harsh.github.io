About me

I am a PhD Research Scholar in the Department of Astronomy & Astrophysics at TIFR, Mumbai under the supervision of Dr. Suvodip Mukherjee. My work mostly focuses on probing Fundamental physics from the Cosmic Microwave Background at small scales. I did my Masters in Physics from IIT Kharagpur in 2022 and Bachelors in Mechanical Engineering from IIT Indore in 2018. You can find my CV here.
