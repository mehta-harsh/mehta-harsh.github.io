Publications

My list of Publications:

  • Modified Cosmology or Modified Galaxy Astrophysics is Driving the z>6 JWST Results? CMB Experiments can discover the Origin in Near Future
    (Mehta & Mukherjee)
    The JWST observations of high-redshift massive galaxies have challenged the current $\Lambda$CDM model of cosmology. We show that a joint analysis involving CMB lensing and patchy-kSZ signals will provide smoking-gun signatures on whether it is the modifications in cosmology or our understanding of astrophysics that is driving the excess in bright galaxies observed by JWST.
  • The prospect from the upcoming CMB experiment LiteBIRD to discover axion-like particles using Milky Way
    (Mehta & Mukherjee)
    The photon-ALP conversion in the Milky way will generate non-Gaussian anisotropies in the CMB at large scales. We show that LiteBIRD will be able to probe the galactic ALP signal and improve the current bounds on the photon-ALP coupling.
